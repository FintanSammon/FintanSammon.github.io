Returns Policy

We offer a full refund of the jersey cost if you are not satisfied with your purchase, as long as you meet certain eligibility criteria.
To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it, with the original packaging and tags still attached. Please include a note with your order number and request when returning the item, or there may be a delay in processing your return.
Before initiating a return, please contact us by email to confirm the correct returns address. Our email address is EireRetroJerseys@gmail.com Please note that we cannot refund items that are damaged or missing tags, or for reasons not related to our error.
Additionally, we cannot accept returns for jerseys that show obvious signs of considerable wear or have a strong odor. Customers are responsible for paying their own shipping costs when returning items. Shipping costs are non-refundable.
If you need to exchange a jersey for a different size, it can be done as long as the item is in the same condition as when you received it, with tags still attached and in its original packaging. Please contact us at EireRetroJerseys@gmail.com if you have a sizing issue.
